Prawn Gnocci Starter

A nice, grown-up appetiser filled with rich creamy sauce

  • Butter
  • Prawns
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Gnocci
  • Garlic Oil
  • Single Cream
  • White Wine (Trebbiano)
  • Spinach
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chopped Parsley
  • Small Garlic Bread
  • Lemon Wedge
  1. Pre heat oven, once hot place small garlic bread in for recommended ammount of time.
  2. Heat 5 prawns, 8 cherry tomato halves and 8 gnocci pieces in a pan with a teaspoon of butter.
  3. When beggining to brown, add a tea spoon of garlic oil and briefly cook for 45 seconds.
  4. Add 2 oz of single cream and 2 oz of white wine to the mix.
  5. Add spinach.
  6. Add seasoning: Salt, pepper and chopped parlsey.
  7. Mix together well
  8. Take garlic bread out and cut into small pieces (I recommend triangular shapes!)
  9. Plate up the sauce into a small bowl, place on a plate with the fresh lemon wedge and garlic bread.
  10. Enjoy!
