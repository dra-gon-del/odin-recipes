Penne Arrabbiata

Deliciously saucy and spicey; served with freshly prepared penne and red chilli garnish.

  • Penne Pasta
  • Pomodoro Sauce
  • Garlic Chilli Paste
  • Chopped Parsley
  • Salt
  • Hard Cheese
  • Red Chilli Garnish
  • Sprig of Parsley
  1. Add dry penne portion into a pan of boiling water for the required 15-20 minutes.
  2. Add 4 oz of pomodoro sauce, tea spoon of garlic chilli paste to a pan and mix.
  3. Once bubbling, add pinch of salt and pinch of parsley to the sauce and mix.
  4. Once ready, filter penne out with the collinder (safety first!), add to the sauce and mix.
  5. Plate up the pasta neatly with a clean pair of cooking tongs.
  6. Add hard cheese, chilli garnish and sprig of parsley for the garnish.
  7. Enjoy! And remember the wash up!
