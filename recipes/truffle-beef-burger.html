Truffle Beef Burger

A beautifully composed burger, a delicious burrata ball squeezed between two juicy beef burgers; served with calabrese ketchup and truffle fries topped with hard cheese and parlsey!"

  • 2 Beef Burger Patties
  • 2 Bread Buns
  • Truffle Mayo
  • Calabrese Ketchup
  • Rocket
  • Burrata Ball
  • Chips/Fries
  • Hard Cheese
  • Chopped Parsley
  • Truffle Oil
  • Burger Serving Skewer
  • Sun Blush Tomato
  • Olive
  1. Place two burger patties on a grill or pan and heat up till the temperature is 75 degrees or over (82 degrees if in Scotland).
  2. Fry or oven cook fries or chips for the recommended times.
  3. Toast buns on the grill or in a separate pan till light brown.
  4. Squirt a blob of truffle mayo on both buns.
  5. Grab a handful of rocket and share between the two buns.
  6. Once the burgers are to temperature, divide the burrata ball into 2 quarters and put one in the between the two burgers on top of one another and the other on top of the whole pile.
  7. Assemple the whole burger together with the burger patties and buns.
  8. Place skewer (with is sun blush tomato and olive stabbed through) through the whole burger to keep it in tact.
  9. Take chips and top them with truffle oil, hard cheese and chopped parsley.
  10. Place the burger and chips on a plate, and serve with a portion of calabrese ketchup on the side.
  11. Enjoy!
